Unlike other auto injury claims, investigations into bus injury claims can be lengthy and complicated. Factors like who owns the bus company and what state the buses are registered in can drag an investigation out. ## Case Settlement

Bus accidents can be complicated for a few reasons. First, the entity that owns the bus determines who can be held liable and whether or not there are any damage caps. A crash with a public transportation bus would mean that the driver is a government employee and therefore a suit would have to follow procedures associated with carrying out a lawsuit against the government. If the bus is privately owned, cases may be simpler to resolve, depending on who owns the bus and what state the bus is registered in, and would follow the procedures that one would normally follow in the case of a personal injury or negligence case.
